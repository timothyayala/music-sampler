Riley Green is a popular artist on his way to country stardom. Hailing from Jacksonville, Alabama, Green has a variety of different albums, EP's and singles, and was voted New Male Country Artist of the Year by the Academy of Country Music in 2019. His mix of slow and fast styles and his vocal range are just a couple of aspects of his work that make him as successful as he is.
